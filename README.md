# Punktured Art — Tattoo Studio Website

**Live Site:** [punkturedart.com](https://punkturedart.com)

**Project Type:** Client Website — Webflow Design & Development

**Role:** Web Designer & Developer

**Timeline:** 2025 – 2026

**Location:** West Kelowna, BC

---

## The Brief

Punktured Art is a tattoo studio based in West Kelowna, BC (3645 Gossett Rd #212) with a 5.0 Google rating. They needed a website that matched the bold, dark, and artistic identity of their brand — something that could attract new clients, showcase artist portfolios, and make it easy for people to book consultations.

They had no existing web presence to speak of. The goal was to go from zero to a fully functional, visually compelling site that felt true to the tattoo culture and aesthetic their artists represent.

---

## What I Built

- Full site design and development in **Webflow**
- Individual **artist profile pages** with gallery lightbox, bio, and direct social links
- Full **portfolio gallery** with lightbox image viewer and thumbnail navigation
- Services and pricing overview page
- Contact and **"Book a Visit"** consultation flow with strategic CTA placement
- Fully **mobile-first responsive** layouts across all pages
- Brand-aligned visual design: dark palette, bold editorial typography, high-contrast imagery
- **Google Maps location embed** with business address for local discoverability
- **Instagram backlinking** per artist profile to drive cross-platform traffic and booking inquiries
- **Local SEO setup**: structured business metadata, Google Business-aligned page structure, clean URL architecture

## Technical Details

- **Custom embed code** — wrote and implemented Google Maps embed directly into Webflow for accurate location display and local SEO benefit.
- **Social media backlinking** — implemented per-artist Instagram links to connect the site to each artist’s active social following.
- **Gallery lightbox** — built full-screen image viewer with thumbnail strip navigation for portfolio browsing.
- **CTA architecture** — "Book a Visit" and "View Pricing" CTAs placed at strategic points throughout the site to drive consultation conversions.
- **Mobile-first build** — primary audience browses on mobile, so the entire layout was designed and tested mobile-first before desktop.
- **Local SEO** — page titles, meta descriptions, address schema, and Google Maps integration structured to support Google Business visibility.

---

## My Process

1. **Discovery** — Researched the local Kelowna tattoo market and reviewed competitor sites to identify gaps and opportunities.
2. **Brand alignment** — Built a visual language for the web that matched the existing brand identity: dark, bold, and editorial.
3. **Wireframing** — Mapped out page structure and user flows before moving into design.
4. **Design & Build** — Designed and developed entirely in Webflow, iterating based on client feedback.
5. **Technical integrations** — Wrote custom embed code for Google Maps, implemented Instagram backlinks per artist, and configured local SEO metadata.
6. **Launch** — Delivered a live, indexed site ready for organic traffic and client bookings.
