# Punktured Art — Tattoo Studio Website

**Live Site:** [punkturedart.com](https://punkturedart.com)

**Project Type:** Client Website — Webflow Design & Development

**Role:** Web Designer & Developer

**Location:** Kelowna, BC

---

## The Brief

Punktured Art is a tattoo studio based in Kelowna, BC. They needed a website that matched the bold, dark, and artistic identity of their brand — something that could attract new clients, showcase artist portfolios, and make it easy for people to book consultations.

They had no existing web presence to speak of. The goal was to go from zero to a fully functional, visually compelling site that felt true to the tattoo culture and aesthetic their artists represent.

---

## What I Built

- Full site design and development in **Webflow**
- Artist portfolio and gallery pages to showcase work
- Services and pricing overview
- Contact and booking inquiry flow
- Fully **mobile-responsive** layouts across all pages
- Brand-aligned visual design: dark palette, bold typography, editorial feel
- SEO basics: page titles, meta descriptions, clean URL structure

---

## My Process

1. **Discovery** — Researched the local Kelowna tattoo market and reviewed competitor sites to identify gaps and opportunities.
2. **Brand alignment** — Worked with the existing Punktured Art brand identity to build a visual language for the web that felt authentic.
3. **Wireframing** — Mapped out page structure and user flows before moving into design.
4. **Design & Build** — Designed and developed entirely in Webflow, iterating based on client feedback.
5. **Launch** — Delivered a live, indexed site ready for organic traffic and client inquiries.
